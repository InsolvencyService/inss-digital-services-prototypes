Insolvency practioner

Use this form if your insolvency practitioner:

  • failed to meet professional standards, for example actions contrary to Statements of Insolvency Practice, Code of Ethics
  • failed to meet ethical standards, for example conflicts of interest
  • provided dissatisfactory customer service, for example a breakdown in communication with the IP

We won’t consider:

  • complaints relating to legal matters (for example, failure to follow sections of the legislation such as the Insolvency Act)
  • complaints relating to commercial matters (for example, decisions reached regarding asset sales or creditor claims)
