Complain about an Insolvency Practitioner

Before completing this form, read our guidance on complaining about an insolvency practitioner.We can only send your complaint onto the IPs authorising body if:

  • you have already complained to the IP directly
  • your complaint has not already been assessed by the authorising body
  • your complaint meets the criteria for referral

If you are complaining about the Insolvency Service, go back and choose complain and about the Insolvency Service option
