Before you complete this form

Before you complete this form, you must read our guidance (opens in new tab) on complaining about an insolvency practitioner.
We will consider your complaint based on the evidence you provide. Please note that we can only send your complaint onto the Insolvency Practitioner's authorising body if:

  • you have already complained to the Insolvency Practitioner directly and you can evidence this
  • your complaint has not already been assessed by the authorising body or the Gateway
  • your complaint meets the criteria for referral listed in our guidance (opens in new tab)
  • you have provided evidence to support your claims

If you are complaining about the Insolvency Service, we have a separate complaints procedure (opens in new tab).

Please provide a copy of the IP complaint and their response as supporting evidence.
