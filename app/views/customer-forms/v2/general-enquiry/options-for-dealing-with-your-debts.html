Options for dealing with your debts

f you owe people money (your ‘creditors’) you can make arrangements to pay your debts. Your options depend on the amount of money and assets you have.

Where you can get help

Speak to a debt adviser to get help choosing the best way to deal with your debt. MoneyHelper has information about debt management and free debt advisory services.

Money advisors offer independent advice to tailer a solution that suits your circumstances, they may offer any of the following solutions to help you deal with your depts.

  • Breathing Space(Dept Respite Scheme)
  • Debt Management Plans
  • Administration orders
  • Individual Voluntary Arrangements
  • Debt Relief Orders
