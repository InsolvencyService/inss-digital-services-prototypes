Supporting evidence

The Gateway can only consider complaints based on the information and evidence that you provide. If you do not provide any evidence, it is unlikely that your complaint can be properly assessed and referred to the IP’s authorising body.

Please note that in most cases, the authorising body will only be able to uphold your complaint if you are able to provide evidence which supports the allegations you have made against the insolvency practitioner.

For a disciplinary investigation to be taken forward by the authorising body, there must be a clear indication of wrongdoing by an insolvency practitioner (or those working under the direction of an IP).

Please upload all evidence that you believe supports your complaint, e.g: * any agreements between you and the IP (IVA agreements, settlement agreements, etc) * copies of correspondence (emails, letters, messages, etc) * a copy of your complaint to the IP * the IP’s response to your complaint * if your complaint is about an IVA, please also provide a copy of the IVA proposal and chairman's report

Your evidence can be uploaded using the Choose File buttons below. Alternatively, please post any hard copies to … (address)
