Investigations and enforcement services {{ data['organisation'] }}

You can make a complaint if you have reasonable grounds to suspect a company that is currently active or dissolved of:

  • causing significant harm to groups such as customers and suppliers
  • breaking the law, for example fraud
  • serious misconduct, for example company assets have not been used properly
  • having a significant irregularity in its affairs

Before you complete this form please read Company Investigations: what we do (opens in a new tab). If your report is about a dissolved company please read our dissolved company investigations guidance (opens in a new tab).

If your complaint is about an active company or business using the name (or similar) of a company that is in liquidation, go back and select investigations and enforcement services breach questionnaire as your reason for contact.

You can find the company's number and its status using the Companies House Register(opens in a new tab).
