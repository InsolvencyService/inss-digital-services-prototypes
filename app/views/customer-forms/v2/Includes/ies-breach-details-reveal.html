Disqualified director

You can use this form if a disqualified director :

  • is acting in the management of a UK company
  • is acting in the management of a foreign company that has sufficient connection to the UK, even if it is not UK registered
  • is taking part in the promotion or formation of a company

Individual that is subject to bankruptcy or Debt Relief restrictions

You can use this form if a Individual that is subject to bankruptcy or debt relief restrictions:

  • may have committed criminal offences or other regulatory breaches
  • where the case may be referred to the criminal investigation team (opens in new tab), the police, a regulator or other investigation agency
  • where you want to submit an anonymous complaint
